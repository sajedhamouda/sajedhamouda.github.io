I'm Sajed Hamouda — a Wix Certified designer and founder of Da Vinci Studio, based in Abu Dhabi, UAE. With over 6 years of experience building websites from scratch, I specialize in creating elegant, high-performance digital experiences for brands that refuse to settle for average.

My background is anything but ordinary. As both a trained dental technician and a certified web designer, I bring an obsessive attention to detail to every project — whether it's a luxury kitchen showroom, a dental laboratory, or a premium lifestyle brand. I understand my clients' industries because I've worked in them.

What sets my work apart is simple: I don't stop until it's right. From architecture and typography to multilingual support and logo design, every element is crafted with precision — because your digital presence deserves nothing less.
